Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

Virtue is a digital wellbeing app that helps users control app addiction by blocking distracting apps like YouTube, Instagram, and others. We provide flexible options such as premium subscription or ad-based access so you can stay productive and mindful of your usage.

2. What Data We Collect

a) You Provide: Name, email, phone number, and login details when you sign in using Google, Facebook, or mobile login.

b) We Collect Automatically: Device details, app usage statistics (only related to blocking features), and anonymized crash logs for improving performance.

3. Why We Collect This Data

We use your data to provide app-blocking functionality, manage your subscription or ad-based access, and ensure a secure login experience. We never sell your data.

4. How We Use and Share Information

Your data is used only to enable Virtue’s features. We may share limited information with trusted partners (like payment providers or ad networks) to deliver subscriptions or ad experiences. We do not share your data for unrelated purposes.

5. Data Security

Your data is encrypted and protected with strict security measures. However, no system is fully immune to risks, so please use secure login credentials and avoid sharing them.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as you use Virtue or as required for subscription and legal compliance. If you uninstall the app, most data is deleted automatically.

7. Children’s Privacy

Virtue is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support. You may also withdraw consent by uninstalling the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Any changes will be communicated inside the app and via updated version notes.
